StAnza, the poetry festival based in St Andrews, will be mid-March and is now
selling tickets.

From Friday the 14th til Sunday the 16th, there will be a wide variety of
talks, readings, open mics, organised walks, and more besides, with English,
Gaelic and Scots all represented.

You can now view the [StAnza programme][programme] or [buy tickets][tickets].
