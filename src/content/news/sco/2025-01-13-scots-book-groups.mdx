Open Book is an organisation that rins online readin and scrievin warkshops.
Lest year, they stertit a couple o regular Scots-leid anes. Their aim is tae
bigg community and fecht laneliness bi skairin, and scrievin, poems and
stories.

In Scots, they rin a Creative Writing warkshop, and a Shared Reading warkshop,
baith ance a munth on Zoom. Aa their sessions require registration, but ar
completely free.

[This month's Creative Writing session][creative-writing-eventbrite] will be
the day at 11am, and [this month's Shared Reading session][shared-reading-eventbrite]
will be on the 18th at 11am.

To finnd out mair about Open Book, visit the [Open Book website][open-book], or
[sign up til their newsletter][open-book-subscribe] for tae hear regular email
updates about upcomin events.
