title: Sangschaw winners 2024
summary: Annooncin the winners o the 2024 Sangschaw!
---

We’re delitit tae annoonce the winners o the 2024 Sangschaw competeition! There
wis mair submeissions nor iver afore, and the qualitie o submeissions was heicher
nor iver afore! Aw the winnin entries, and some o the rinners-up, will be
furthset i furthcomin edeitions o _Lallans_.
